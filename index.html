BANGA SOUP


ABOUT BANGA SOUP

Banga Soup is a Nigerian soup that is native to the Southernarts of Nigeria. It is very similar to the Igbo’s Ofe Akwu, but the additional spices used for preparing Banga Soup, makes it differ in taste and aroma.

Banga soup is known by several names it is called izuwo amediizuwo amedi in Urhobo language and izuwo edi in Isoko language

Here’s the recipe.. Enjoy!


INGREDIENTS MEASUREMENT
Palm Fruits 500g
Asorted Meat 500g
Fish 3
Banga Spice 1 tablespoon
Oborunbebe stick 2
Onion 1
Ground crayfish 1 tablespoon
Seasoning 1 cube
Pepper 1 tablespoon
Salt To taste
Ground crayfish 1 tablespoon
Perewincle 3 cups

PREPARATION STEPS

  • Wash and boil your banga until the skin is soft
  • Pound the banga in a morta, wash and strain to get the extract
  • Pour the extract in a pot and start cooking
  • Add Meat, fish and allow to boil
  • Add onions, pepper, ground cray fish, salt and seasoning
  • Add oburunbebe stick
  • Add beletete leave
  • Add spices
  • Add perewincle
  • Allow to cook and your banga soup is ready
  • Serve with hot starch for maximum enjoyment
